Integrations

BionicGPT gives all your teams access to no code RAG

Team members can try out Generative AI on their documents

When they are happy with a prompt and data combination they can deploy it to other team members

Bionic works on the principal that each team know best which data to make available and what the results should look like.

Each team can create and manage their own data sets.

Data sets are filled with documents from multiple sources.

80% of enterprise data exists in difficult-to-use formats like HTML, PDF, CSV, PNG, PPTX, and more.

BionicGPT effortlessly extracts and transforms complex data and makes it available to the AI.

We also support for optical character recognition (coming soon).

Role based access controls maintain data confidentiality

Teams manage themselves and can invite new members. No need to wait for IT.

Teams have the ability to manage their own permissions

Data is private to the team

Teams can manage themselves and invite colleagues.

Bionic scales from Local LLM's running on a laptop to infinity and beyond.

We have connectors for pretty much every LLM solution on the market

The pipeline waits for documents to change and automatically adds them to our vector database.
